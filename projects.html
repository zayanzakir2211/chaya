Scholarship:

Education is one of the most powerful tools for personal and social development.
It provides opportunities and development of knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in personal life and contribute to society.

  • Project duration and coverage:
    • From 2015 –till now
    • About 3000 students have been given scholarships of schools, colleges and universities Student.

Pre-primary school:

CHAYA conducts pre-primary school programs to make school-oriented children especially
those who do not have access to school or do not attend school due to a distance from their houses.

  • Project duration and coverage:
    • From 2015 –till now
    • We had two pre-primary schools running.

Safe drinking water:

We believe that providing safe water for all is essential for human survival and environmental balance.
And for this purpose, we conduct awareness activities among people about the importance of safe water and water pollution.

  • Project duration and coverage:
    • From 2015 –till now
    • Tube wells have been distributed to a total of 410 families and 115 educational and religious institutions.
    • Apart from this, a total of 150 awareness programs about clean water have been conducted at various union and ward levels.

Distribution of winter clothes:

CHAYA distributes winter clothes to the needy people during the winter season as a kind and charitable act, which helps to keep them warm and comfortable.
And for this purpose, we conduct awareness activities among people about the importance of safe water and water pollution.

  • Project duration and coverage:
    • From 2015 –till now.
    • A total of 3500 blankets have been distributed to the poor, disabled, widows and elderly people.

Relief activities:

Relief activities are essential during crisis or natural calamities to provide help to victims of various natural calamities.

  • Project duration and coverage:
    • From 2015 –till now.
    • Relief has been distributed to a total of 1,000 families during floods.

Emergency support:

    • An immediate assistance program is a program that works to provide emergency assistance to individuals or communities facing unexpected or emergency situations.
    • Our organization conducts activities such as cash payment for medical treatment,
    ‎ ‎ construction of houses for people suddenly made homeless due to cyclone and fire accidents as part of the immediate relief program.
