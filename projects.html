Our Projects

Making a difference through education, healthcare, and community support

01

Scholarship Program

Our scholarship program supports meritorious students from underprivileged families, enabling them to continue their education. We provide study materials and academic guidance to help students achieve their dreams.

Students Supported

02

Pre-Primary Schools

We run pre-primary schools in remote char areas where children have no access to early education. These schools provide foundational learning, preparing young children for formal education and giving them a strong start in life.

03

Safe Drinking Water

Access to clean drinking water is a basic necessity. We install tube wells and water purification systems in communities, schools, and institutions to ensure families have access to safe, arsenic-free drinking water.

410+
Families Served

04

Winter Clothes Distribution

Every winter, we distribute blankets and warm clothing to vulnerable families in remote areas. The harsh winter months can be life-threatening for the poor without adequate protection from the cold.

3500+
Blankets Distributed
Annual

05

Emergency Relief Work

During floods, natural disasters, and emergencies, we provide immediate relief to affected communities. Our relief packages include food, clean water, medicine, and essential supplies to help families survive difficult times. Our organization conducts activities such as cash payment for medical treatment, construction of houses for people suddenly made homeless due to cyclone and fire accidents as part of the immediate relief program.

Sanitation & Hygiene

We build sanitary latrines and promote hygiene awareness in communities where open defecation is common. Proper sanitation prevents disease and improves overall community health.
