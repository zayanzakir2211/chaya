About Us

Building Hope, Changing Lives

In the northern part of Bangladesh in Kurigram district, there are about 5 lakh poor people living in the temporary islands that have developed in the mighty Brahmaputra River and on the banks of swamps.

The overall condition of social, economic, political, educational, health values of these poor people is almost going to be endangered. In order to get out of such a delicate situation, the socio-economic development requires human welfare, education, health, medical care and environment protection.

In this realization, a non-political and voluntary social welfare organization named "CHAYA" (Center for Helping to Activate Youth and Adult) started its activities in Kurigram district on 10th February 2015.

Education

Scholarships and pre-primary schools for underprivileged children

Safe Water

Providing clean drinking water to families and institutions

Healthcare

Medical assistance and sanitation programs

Relief Work

Emergency support during natural disasters

Creating Pathways to Self-Reliance

We believe in empowering communities to build sustainable futures through education, financial services, and direct support during times of need.
